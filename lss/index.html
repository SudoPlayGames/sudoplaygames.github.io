Lodestar: Stygian Skies is a procedural, voxel-based, tactical, sci-fi role-playing game that takes place in the dark, futuristic expanse of the Lodestar Universe. Explore planets, find resources, unlock unit classes, augment your team and engage in turn-based combat on destructible terrain.
